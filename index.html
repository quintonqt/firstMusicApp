Our Mission

At PetalsForPeace, we strive to provide the best flowers that will bring smiles to the ones you love. Our flowers are grown in the richest soil by local farmers in the Georgia community, and they are handled with care from the time they're seeded to the time they arrive in your home. We believe that flowers are nature's gift to us. That's why we want to share this gift with you :)
